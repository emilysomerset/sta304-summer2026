Learning Outcomes
  • Explain why ratio estimation is used.
  • Explain how the ratio and regression estimator adjusts the sample mean based on sampled values of the auxiliary variable
  • Calculate and interpret ratio and regression estimators and their associated bias and variance
  • Construct and interpret confidence intervals for the population mean and totals using the ratio estimator or regression estimator
  • Identify condition under which regression estimation might be preferred over ratio estimation
  • Identify conditions under which ratio estimation might be preferred over the sample mean estimator under SRS
