Learning Outcomes
  • Derive and evaluate the properties (e.g. bias, variance) of estimators (total, proportion, mean) under SRS.
  • Understand how the sampling fraction affects uncertainty of estimators through the finite population correction.
  • Calculate and interpret confidence intervals for population parameters when population variance is either known or unknown.
  • Perform and interpret one-sided and two-sided hypothesis tests for population parameters at a specified significance level.
  • Use asymptotic (large-sample) approximations to sampling distributions of estimators (total, mean, proportion) for calculations such as sample size determination for a specified margin of error.
