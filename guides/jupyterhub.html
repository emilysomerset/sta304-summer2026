JupyterHub

How to access and use the course computing environment on JupyterHub.

Accessing JupyterHub
  1. Go to JupyterHub and log in with your UTORid.
  2. Your personal workspace will load automatically.
  3. Learning activities to be completed in a .qmd worksheet will appear in your sta304-summer2026/lecture_number folder.
Compiling your .qmd file
  1. When done, click the Render button (blue arrow at the top of the editor) to compile to PDF.
  2. Your PDF will appear in the Files pane (bottom right).
  3. Download it by checking the box next to the file → More → Export.
Submitting Your Work

    Submit the compiled PDF to MarkUs by the learning activity deadline.

Need Help?

Post on Piazza or visit office hours. For server issues, contact the CS Help Desk.
