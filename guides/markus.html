MarkUs

Step-by-step instructions for submitting your learning activities on MarkUs.

What is MarkUs?

MarkUs is the submission portal used for all STA304 learning activities. You will upload your completed PDF here before each deadline.

How to Submit
  1. Go to markus.teach.cs.toronto.edu/markus/ and log in with your UTORid.
  2. Select STA304 from the course list.
  3. Click the assignment you want to submit (e.g. LA1).
  4. Click Submit File and upload your file.
  5. Confirm your submission appears under Submitted Files.

⚠️ You can resubmit before the deadline — the most recent file will be graded.

File Format

Submit your learning activities as a PDF. .

Filename convention: sta304_LA1_studentnumber.pdf — e.g. sta304_LA1_1234567.pdf.

Need Help?

If you have trouble logging in or submitting, post on Piazza or attend office hours. Do not email the instructor about MarkUs login issues — visit this info page for more information.
