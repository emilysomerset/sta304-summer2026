Learning Outcomes
  • Understand why stratified sampling is used.
  • Derive and evaluate the properties (e.g. bias, variance) of estimators (total, proportion, mean) under stratified sampling..
  • Calculate and interpret confidence intervals for population parameters when population variance is either known or unknown.
  • Perform and interpret one-sided and two-sided hypothesis tests for population parameters at a specified significance level.
  • Explain why stratified sampling improves on SRS.
  • Implement and compare proportional and optimal allocation.
  • Evaluate the tradeoffs between proportional and optimal allocation in stratified sampling.
